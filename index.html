Welcome to The Stump Farm

Nestled in the heart of upstate New York, The Stump Farm is a family-run venture born from a passion for sustainable agriculture and wholesome food. We believe that the best meat comes from animals raised ethically, with plenty of space to roam and forage naturally.

Our chickens and turkeys are raised on lush pastures, enjoying a life full of sunshine and fresh air. This not only ensures their well-being but also results in meat that is more flavorful, tender, and nutritious. We're proud to offer our community a direct connection to their food source.
